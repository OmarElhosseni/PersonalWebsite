Coder & PC Enthusiast

About Me

I am an Egyptian-born student who loves to learn, create, and grow. My first time using a computer was when I was living in Egypt and my father bought a computer so that my mother and I could video call him over the internet while he worked in America. Pretty much ever since then I've been obsessed with computers.

What I Like

One of my most prevalent interests is PC hardware. I love learning about all the new advancements in consumer PCs and have built my own computer as a testament to this.

I have always loved to come up with ideas and bring them to life. So, unsurprisingly, when I learned to code, I instantly fell in love with it. It allows me to connect my passion for learning and passion for computers
